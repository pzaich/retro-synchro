# Swimmer Sprite Plan

All sprites should be 64x64 pixel art PNGs with transparent backgrounds.
Use `swimmer-default` as an example reference for all other art styles.

Save to `public/assets/sprites/`.

## Current sprites (3)

| File | Pose | Used for |
|---|---|---|
| `swimmer-default.png` | Standing/idle | Fallback, formations |
| `swimmer-lift.png` | Arms out wide, legs together | Lift flyers |
| `swimmer-figure.png` | Arms raised up | Figure performers |

## High priority (6 sprites)

### swimmer-tuck.png
- **Pose**: Knees pulled to chest, compact ball shape
- **Elements**: Tub, Inverted Tuck, Back Tuck Somersault, Oyster

### swimmer-pike.png
- **Pose**: Body folded at hips, legs straight (like touching toes)
- **Elements**: Back Pike, Front Pike, Submerged Back Pike, Barracuda

### swimmer-split.png
- **Pose**: Legs in full split (front or side)
- **Elements**: Split Position, Barracuda Airborne Split, Rocket Split, Scissors Kick

### swimmer-vertical.png
- **Pose**: Body straight and inverted, legs pointing up out of water
- **Elements**: Vertical, Pencil, Tower, Porpoise, Swordfish

### swimmer-spin.png
- **Pose**: Streamlined body mid-twist/rotation blur
- **Elements**: All 13 spin elements (Spin 180, Twist 360, Corkscrew, Log Roll, Helicopter, etc.)

### swimmer-scull.png
- **Pose**: Flat/streamlined, arms at sides, gliding horizontally
- **Elements**: All 14 scull elements (Torpedo, Alligator, Dolphin Kick, etc.)

## Medium priority (4 sprites)

### swimmer-ballet-leg.png
- **Pose**: On back in water, one leg extended straight up vertical
- **Elements**: Ballet Leg, Double Ballet Leg, Flamingo, Crane

### swimmer-arch.png
- **Pose**: Back arched, forming a C-curve
- **Elements**: Surface Arch, Front Walkover, Walkout Back, Mermaid Tail

### swimmer-submerged.png
- **Pose**: Partially visible/faded, body underwater
- **Elements**: Spiral Descent, Vertical Descent, submerge phases of figures

### swimmer-throw.png
- **Pose**: Arms up, body launching upward (airborne/explosive)
- **Elements**: Airborne Throw, Catapult Throw, Tower Launch, Airborne Jump

## Lower priority (3 sprites)

### swimmer-starfish.png

- **Elements**: Starfish Float, Blossom, celebration moments

### swimmer-flamingo.png
- **Pose**: One leg bent at knee, other leg extended straight
- **Elements**: Flamingo, Flamingo Combined 720, Bent Knee (Heron)

### swimmer-somersault.png
